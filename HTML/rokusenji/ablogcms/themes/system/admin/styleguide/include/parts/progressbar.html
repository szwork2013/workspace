Progress bar

JavaScriptなどを使って .acms-progress-bar の幅を書き換えてください。

通常
.acms-progress-bar-info
.acms-progress-bar-danger
.acms-progress-striped
.acms-active(animation) 
<div class="acms-progress">
	<div class="acms-progress-bar" style="width: 90%">
		<span>通常</span>
	</div>
</div>

<div class="acms-progress">
	<div class="acms-progress-bar acms-progress-bar-info" style="width: 90%">
		<span>.acms-progress-bar-info</span>
	</div>
</div>

<div class="acms-progress">
	<div class="acms-progress-bar acms-progress-bar-danger" style="width: 90%">
		<span>.acms-progress-bar-danger</span>
	</div>
</div>

<div class="acms-progress acms-progress-striped">
	<div class="acms-progress-bar acms-progress-bar-info" style="width: 90%">
		<span>.acms-progress-striped</span>
	</div>
</div>

<div class="acms-progress acms-progress-striped acms-active">
	<div class="acms-progress-bar acms-progress-bar-info" style="width: 90%">
		<span>.acms-active(animation)</span>
	</div>
</div>
